About Me

I am postdoctoral scholar in the Department of Computational Medicine at University of California, Los Angeles, advised by Kenneth Lange. I received my Ph.D. in Biomathematics at UCLA in March 2021 under the supervision of Kenneth Lange and Dr. Mary Sehl. I also obtained a B.S. in Mathematics/Applied Science from UCLA in June 2013.
