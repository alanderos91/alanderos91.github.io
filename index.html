About Me

I am an Assitant Professor in the Department of Statistics at University of California, Riverside. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Department of Computational Medicine at University of California, Los Angeles, advised by Kenneth Lange. My alma mater was very kind to me. I received my Ph.D. in Biomathematics at UCLA in March 2021 under the supervision of Kenneth Lange and Dr. Mary Sehl. I also obtained a B.S. in Mathematics/Applied Science from UCLA in June 2013, with a specialization in computing.
